Who Are We?

1) Who Are We #DevUps is a newly formed tech society of KIET Group of Institutions working towards matching the skilset of students with the corporate standards. #Our club helps the students to know about their right goals and strengthening their skill set to match the competition in the industry. #The Club is for the students and by the students. So the club provides the platform for the students to learn and the platform where they can put their ideas easily and the club will help to nurture them #The Club's pace matches the today's world IT Sector's Fast changing environment and acceptability towards new tools and domains. #Our Club helps in enhancing the practical knowlede of the students along with their traditional studies
